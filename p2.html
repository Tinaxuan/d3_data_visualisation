Alcohol Dependence in the USA

National Survey on Drug Use and Health

About this visualisation

This visualisation is about alcohol dependence among different age groups and states. Data was collected from individual states as part of the NSDUH study which ranges from 2010 to 2014.


The line graph on the right shows the trends over the five year span for the whole of the USA. Each line represents a different age group as shown by the key.

The map and the pie chart below focus on the totals of each state. The pie chart reflects the breakdown for each state when selected on the map.
